About Us

Kizomba is a genre of dance and a musical genre originating in Angola in 1984. Kizomba means "party" in Kimbundu, a Bantu language spoken in Angola. Kizomba is characterised by a slower, romantic, and sensuous rhythm. Kizomba is growing in popularity and we want to do our part in bringing this fun dance to as many people as possible.

Our Company, Kizomba, was founded in 2015. We organize festivals and dance parties in all the major cities of the world. Whether you are a pro or an amateur we cater to all. We keep our dance parties reasonably small to create a more intimate relationships with our clients and to make the learning experience much more enjoyable. Our experienced instructors are patient and want you to have fun. We invite you all to join.

About US

We have been organizing Kizomba festivals for 5 years now. Our mission is to raise awareness of this dance that is taking the world by storm one dance festival at a time. We want to spread the joy of kizomba.
